Mission and Vision

GAMEC is a non-profit 501(c)(3) voluntary membership association of organisations individuals whose mission is to be of service to Allah (swt). Our mission is to create a supportive and inclusive environment where Muslim Eritreans around the world can connect, collaborate, and thrive. We are committed to preserving our rich cultural heritage, nurturing strong Islamic values, and building bridges of understanding.

Programs and Initiatives

Our programs and initiatives form the heart of GAMEC's dedication to uplifting our community. From engaging educational workshops and empowering social welfare projects to vibrant cultural events and enlightening interfaith dialogues, our diverse range of initiatives aims to inspire, educate, and unite.
