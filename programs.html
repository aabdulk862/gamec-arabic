Programs

Discover the diverse and enriching programs offered by the Global Association of Muslim Eritrean Communities (GAMEC). Our programs are thoughtfully designed to empower, educate, and foster unity within our community, catering to individuals of all ages and interests.

Matrimonial Services

Our Matrimonial Program is dedicated to fostering strong and harmonious relationships within the Muslim Eritrean community. We provide resources, support, and events to help individuals and families navigate the journey of finding compatible partners based on shared values, faith, and cultural backgrounds.
